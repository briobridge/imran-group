Our Heritage

A Legacy of Distinction

IG Veritas et Industria

LAHORE · GULBERG · MCMLXXII

  • Integrity in all undertakings
  • Excellence without compromise
  • Legacy over immediacy
  • Community & stewardship

Founded in 1972 amidst the burgeoning commercial landscape of Lahore, the Imran Group of Companies has evolved from a singular mercantile venture into one of Punjab's most respected conglomerates. Our founding patriarch established this enterprise upon the unshakeable conviction that commerce, conducted with honour, is the highest form of public service.

"A man's business is a mirror of his character — and ours shall reflect only the finest."

Over five decades, the Group has navigated economic vicissitudes, political transitions, and market upheavals with a composure born of deep institutional knowledge. Each subsidiary under our banner has been cultivated with patience, guided by principle, and staffed by professionals of the highest calibre.

From the premium real estate developments that define Lahore's skyline, to the technological ventures shaping the commerce of tomorrow, the Imran Group continues its tradition of dignified, purposeful enterprise — ever mindful that what we build today forms the inheritance of those who follow.

Real Estate & Development

Imran Properties

A venerable name in Lahore's property landscape, Imran Properties develops, manages, and brokers real estate of singular distinction. Our portfolio encompasses commercial towers in Gulberg and DHA, luxury residential communities, and curated retail spaces. We do not merely build structures — we craft the enduring environments in which legacies are lived.

International Trade

Imran Trading Co.

The original cornerstone of the Imran Group, our trading house has, for over half a century, maintained commercial relationships across the subcontinent, the Gulf States, and Europe. Specialising in commodities, textiles, and industrial goods, Imran Trading Co. commands respect born of reliability and a network cultivated through generations of honourable dealing.

Est. 1975

Hospitality & Leisure

Crescent Hospitality

Purveyors of refined hospitality, Crescent operates boutique hotels and exclusive banqueting facilities in Lahore and Islamabad. Our establishments are distinguished by an unwavering attention to detail — from the thread count of our linens to the provenance of our cuisine — catering to a clientele for whom comfort and decorum are non-negotiable.

Est. 1995

Financial Services

Gulberg Capital

The Group's financial services arm, Gulberg Capital provides bespoke investment advisory, asset management, and private equity solutions to high-net-worth individuals and institutional clients. Leadership

The Steward

MI Chairman

Mr. Imran Ahmed

Founder & Chairman — Imran Group of Companies

A man forged in the finest traditions of Lahori enterprise, Mr. Imran Ahmed has dedicated five decades to the building of an institution that bears not merely a name, but a philosophy. Educated at Government College Lahore and subsequently at the London School of Economics, he returned to his homeland with a vision that has since shaped the commercial landscape of Punjab. Under his stewardship, the Group has grown from a solitary trading house to a diversified conglomerate — always, as he insists, "brick by patient brick."
